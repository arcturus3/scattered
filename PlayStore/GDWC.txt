Game name: Scattered
Team name: AS Games
Team members: Arti Schmidt
Category: HOBBY

Description: Keep track of a ball in VR bouncing around in a room filled with ones that all look the same. See how much you can increase the difficulty before it becomes impossible. A menu with restart and return home options can be found during a game by looking straight down.

Hardware requirements: Google Cardboard VR viewer, phone running Android 4.4 or higher, screen size between 4 and 6 inches.
A viewer can be found here: https://store.google.com/product/google_cardboard

Install and usage instructions: Install from Google Play here: https://play.google.com/store/apps/details?id=com.atschmidt.Scattered
Launch the app, and place phone in a Google Cardboard VR viewer. Selection is performed via the click button on the viewer.
